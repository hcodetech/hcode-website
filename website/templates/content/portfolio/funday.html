FunDay 

Funday makes local merchant inventory available at public beaches, so you can make the most of your beach day.

On-demand food, beverage, and beach essentials delivered to your location on the beach.

To enable this app uses a smart combination of geo location, an image of the surroundings and some notes to identify the exact location of the user on the beach. All of this done in a seamless experience for the end user. The app was built in django and react-native. We did the first release in 4 weeks and releasing updates every 2 weeks.

The app is currently in private beta.
