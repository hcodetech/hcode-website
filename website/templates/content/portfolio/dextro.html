Dextro: Video Understanding Platform

Our founder, Shashank Singla, worked in Dextro for 2 years as part of a 10 member startup to build and scale a video understanding platform.

During that time, Dextro was ​acquired​ by a publicly traded company, AXON, makers of taser.

Main highlights below:
  • Scaled and enhanced the evaluation framework for neural network models, bringing down the time of evaluation from a 1-2 week period to 2 days and built a UI to expose functionalities.
  • Moved complete training pipeline from a single machine Caffe setup to cloud multi machine Tensorflow setup.
  • Built an end to end video classification engine for pilot with LAPD body worn camera video
