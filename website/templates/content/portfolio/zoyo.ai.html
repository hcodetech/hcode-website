ZoyoAI: NLP Based Chat Bot for shopping

We built an artificial intelligence-based platform that allows users to shop and perform e-commerce transactions over chat. Chat and tell Zoyo what you are looking for. Zoyo will suggest products based on your preferences. Zoyo could also tell you latest news, imdb ratings, horoscope, translations, weather, PNR status etc.


Zoyo was ​acquired​ by MagicTiger, and then we helped MagicTiger release a new app called magicX.


MagicX app (which was a rebranded zoyo app) topped the charts on GooglePlay in the first week of release.
