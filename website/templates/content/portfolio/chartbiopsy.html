Chartbiopsy : Healtcare Platform

We worked closely with NYC doctors to creating a HIPPA compliant platform to make healthcare patient centric. Chartbiopsy is a medical data contextualization service by physicians who bring all the medical data of a patient together to enable patient centric healthcare.

The prototype included:
  • the platform is powered by Django,
  • the frontend design in HTML5 (responsive with grid layout system),
  • integrated APIs like SrFax, Stripe
  • HIPPA compliant end-to-end secure solution

We also created a Virtual Server for all the compilation and contextualization work to keep the solution fully secure.
