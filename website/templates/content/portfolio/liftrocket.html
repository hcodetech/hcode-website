LiftRocket 

LiftRocket helps people raise micro credit to meet life's needs without a bank, paperwork, or credit scores. Fix your car, meet a medical expense, or replace a smartphone.

LiftRocket had a web app built which they wanted ported to an android and ios app. We used react native and their existing apis to build the app.

We did this without any mobile specifi designs and used the reponsive designs and inspiration from other apps to complete the app in 2 months. The app included integrations with Plaid for crediting and debiting money from users bank account and also integration with stripe to charge users credit card.

The app is currently available to download on playstore and in private beta on apple store.
