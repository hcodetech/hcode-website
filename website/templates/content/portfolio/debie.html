Debie : Rating Platform

We created the real-estate ratings platform for startup Debie. Debie is a rating platform for property-owners and tenants unlocking more efficient transactions in real estate.

The platfrom included:
  • property owner in Django,
  • the tenant onboarding flow design in HTML5 (responsive with grid layout system),
  • integrated APIs like plaid,
  • sophisticated rating system

We also enabled various A/B experiemnts for enabling iterations to the right product market fit.
  • Django
  • HTML5
  • Plaid
