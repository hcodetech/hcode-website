Pollinate Art : Discover, Experience & Find Inspiration

Pollinate is a community platform to make art discovery simple and fun. Pollinate empowers artists, curators, and art lovers with new tools to promote events, activate their communities, and connect in real life.

We worked closely with Pollinate’s team of founders and designers to define and develop the product roadmap for the minimum viable product (MVP) and beyond.

The app was built in django and react-native.

The app is available on apple store and playstore for download.
